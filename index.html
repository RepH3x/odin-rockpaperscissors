Rock, Paper, Scissors

Click on a button to pick your move, rock, paper, or scissors. A computer will randomly pick between the moves to oppose your choice. Rock beats scissors, paper beats rock, and scissors beats paper. Five games will be played, a winner declared, and then you will have the opportunity to reset and play another bout of five games.
